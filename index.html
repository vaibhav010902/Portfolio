About Me

Hi, my name is Vaibhav, and I am an aspiring data analyst and software developer with a strong foundation in Python, Power BI, and SQL. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics Engineering from Madhav Institute of Technology and Science, Gwalior.

I’ve always been fascinated by uncovering insights from data, much like solving intricate puzzles. Whether it’s identifying trends, optimizing processes, or creating impactful visualizations, I thrive on finding patterns that drive informed decisions.

With hands-on experience in projects ranging from sales performance analysis to ride-booking service optimization, I have developed dashboards and data models that simplify complex problems into actionable insights. My ability to blend technical expertise with a problem-solving mindset enables me to craft innovative solutions.

Do you have a data challenge or project in mind? Let’s collaborate! Feel free to reach out to me by email.
